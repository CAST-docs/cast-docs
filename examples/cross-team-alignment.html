Status field difference状态字段差异

Spec规格

1 "status": "paid"
2+"payment_status": "settled"

Implementation实现

1 "status": "paid"
2-"payment_status": missing
  • api/order/response.go:42 - Response assembly path响应组装路径

Impact影响

Impact影响

Consumers cannot distinguish status: paid from fully settled orders, because payment_status is missing.消费者无法区分 status: paid 和已完全结算订单，因为 payment_status 缺失。

Decisions决策

The implementation will add payment_status and keep the existing status field unchanged; the change lands in response.go:42.实现会新增 payment_status，并保持现有 status 字段不变；变更落在 response.go:42.
